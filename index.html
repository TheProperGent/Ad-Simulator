Ad Simulator

Ad Simulator is a free web platform where users watch video advertisements and earn credits. Credits are tracked across six rarity tiers, each with its own drop rate, giving every ad watch the chance to reveal something uncommon.

How It Works

Sign in with your Google account and press the Run Ad button. A rarity roll determines which tier of ad you receive. Watch the ad, collect your credit, and build your personal library of discovered ads. Every ad you watch is saved to your collection, including duplicates tracked with quantity badges.

Rarity Tiers

Each ad watch rolls against six tiers of rarity:

  • Common — 90% chance
  • Uncommon — 7% chance
  • Rare — 2.5% chance
  • Epic — 0.49% chance
  • Legendary — 0.0095% chance
  • Mythic — 0.0001% chance

Leaderboard

A global leaderboard ranks users by lifetime credits earned, unique ads collected, and rarest discoveries. Compare your collection against other users and track your standing across all three categories.

For Advertisers

Ad Simulator offers direct video ad placement across all rarity tiers. Higher rarity ads reach users who have demonstrated consistent, voluntary engagement with the platform. Every impression is logged in real time.
